Privacy Policy

UsedCarzHub Mobile Application

Last Updated: August 9, 2025 | Effective Date: August 9, 2025
Your Privacy Matters to Us. UsedCarzHub ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, process, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application and related services.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect personal information that you voluntarily provide to us, including:

  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, postal address
  • Account Information: Username, password, profile picture
  • Vehicle Information: Details about vehicles you list or inquire about
  • Financial Information: Payment details for premium services (processed securely)
  • Communication Data: Messages, reviews, and feedback you provide

1.2 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: Device model, operating system, unique device identifiers
  • Usage Data: App features used, time spent, interaction patterns
  • Location Data: Precise or approximate location (only with your consent)
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, app version, crash reports
Location Services: Location data helps us show you relevant local listings and improve search results. You can disable location access in your device settings at any time.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use your information for the following purposes:

2.1 Service Provision

  • Create and maintain your account
  • Process and manage vehicle listings
  • Facilitate communication between buyers and sellers
  • Provide customer support and respond to inquiries
  • Process payments and transactions

2.2 Improvement and Analytics

  • Analyze app usage to improve our services
  • Conduct research and development
  • Personalize your app experience
  • Generate aggregate statistics

2.3 Communication

  • Send important service notifications
  • Provide marketing communications (with your consent)
  • Share relevant vehicle recommendations
  • Send security alerts and updates
Marketing Communications: You can opt-out of promotional emails at any time by clicking the unsubscribe link or adjusting your account preferences.

3. Information Sharing and Disclosure

We respect your privacy and do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

3.1 With Your Consent

When you explicitly agree to share information with third parties (e.g., connecting with potential buyers/sellers).

3.2 Service Providers

We work with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in:

  • Cloud hosting and data storage
  • Payment processing
  • Analytics and performance monitoring
  • Customer support services
  • Marketing and advertising platforms

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law, court order, or to:

  • Protect our rights and property
  • Ensure user safety and security
  • Prevent fraud or illegal activities
  • Comply with legal obligations
Important: All third-party service providers are contractually bound to protect your information and use it only for the specified purposes.

4. Third-Party Services and Integrations

Our app integrates with various third-party services that may collect information:

4.1 Analytics Services

  • Google Analytics: Usage statistics and app performance
  • Firebase Analytics: User engagement and crash reporting
  • Mixpanel: User behavior analysis

4.2 Social Media Integration

  • Facebook Login and sharing features
  • Google Sign-In authentication

4.3 Payment Processors

Each third-party service operates under its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their privacy practices.

5. Data Security and Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect your information:

5.1 Technical Safeguards

  • SSL/TLS encryption for data transmission
  • Advanced encryption for data storage
  • Regular security audits and vulnerability assessments
  • Secure server infrastructure with access controls
  • Multi-factor authentication for sensitive operations

5.2 Organizational Measures

  • Limited access to personal data on a need-to-know basis
  • Regular employee training on data protection
  • Incident response procedures
  • Data breach notification protocols
Security Notice: While we implement robust security measures, no system is 100% secure. Please use strong passwords and keep your account credentials confidential.

6. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes outlined in this policy:

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and for 2 years after deletion
  • Transaction Records: Kept for 7 years for legal and tax compliance
  • Communication Data: Stored for 3 years for customer service purposes
  • Analytics Data: Aggregated data may be retained indefinitely
  • Legal Hold: Data may be retained longer if required by law or legal proceedings

After the retention period, we securely delete or anonymize your personal information.

7. Your Privacy Rights

You have several rights regarding your personal information:

7.1 Access and Portability

  • Request a copy of your personal information
  • Download your data in a structured format
  • Receive information about how we process your data

7.2 Correction and Updates

  • Update your account information at any time
  • Correct inaccurate personal data
  • Request completion of incomplete data

7.3 Deletion and Restriction

  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Request erasure of specific information
  • Restrict processing of your data

7.4 Objection and Consent Withdrawal

  • Opt-out of marketing communications
  • Object to data processing for specific purposes
  • Withdraw consent for location tracking
Exercise Your Rights: To exercise any of these rights, please contact our privacy team using the information provided below. We will respond within 30 days.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be processed and stored in countries other than your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including:

  • Adequacy decisions by relevant authorities
  • Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs)
  • Privacy Shield or equivalent frameworks
  • Binding Corporate Rules (BCRs)

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected such information, please contact us immediately, and we will take steps to delete it.

Parental Notice: If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided personal information, please contact our privacy team for immediate assistance.

11. Updates to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect changes in our practices or legal requirements. When we make significant changes:

  • We will update the "Last Updated" date

We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy regularly to stay informed about how we protect your information.
