How to Delete Your Profile

Follow these simple steps to permanently delete your account

1

Open the App Menu

Tap on the menu button (usually located in the top corner of your app screen) to open the navigation menu.

2

Find the Delete Account Option

Scroll down to the bottom of the menu. You should find a Delete Account button at the end of the list.

3

Confirm Account Deletion

Tap on the "Delete Account" button. The app will ask you to confirm this action, as account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone.

Important Warning

Please note that deleting your account is permanent and cannot be undone. This action will:

  • Permanently delete all your profile information
  • Remove all your data, posts, and content
  • Make your username available for others to use
