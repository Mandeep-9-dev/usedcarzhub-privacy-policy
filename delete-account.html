Help & Support — Delete account instructions

How to delete your UsedCarzHub account

You can remove your account directly from the mobile app. Follow the steps below. If you are unable to delete your account from the app, contact our support team and we will process the request for you.

  1. Open the UsedCarzHub app and sign in with your username/email and password.
  2. Go to your Profile — tap the profile icon at the top-right (or bottom navigation).
  3. Open Settings — tap Settings or the gear icon.
  4. Select "Account" from the settings list.
  5. Tap "Delete Account" and follow the on-screen confirmation prompts. You may be asked to re-enter your password for security.
  6. Confirm deletion — after confirming, your account and personal data associated with the account will be scheduled for deletion. Some backup copies may remain for a limited period for legal or technical reasons.
Important: Deleting your account will remove your listings and profile. If you choose to sign up again later, you will need to create a new account.
If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion via support, please use one of the options below:
Email support@usedcarzhub.com
Include your account email and username. We will verify ownership before processing.
Suggested message to send: 
Subject: Account Deletion Request

Hello UsedCarzHub team,

Please delete my account and all associated personal data.

Account email: your-email@example.com
Username: your-username
Reason (optional): I want to remove my account.

Thank you.
Data retention: After deletion we may keep limited backups for up to 30 days for recovery and for legal obligations. For privacy questions, see our Privacy Policy or contact support@usedcarzhub.com.
